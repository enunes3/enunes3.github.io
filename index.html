I am currently 25 years old and I was born on Cape Cod. My parents moved from Brasil about 30 years ago and I am the first generation to be born here in the US. We only spoke Portuguese in the house and my sister and I mixed both Portuguese and English, and I learned basic Spanish later on in school. I am the 5th child out of 6, but the first daughter. Since I was around 5 years old, we started to visit our family regularly in Brasil. I spent my first two years of undergrad in Spain with an American program based out of Madrid. I was able to truly immerse myself by only trying to speak Spanish and asking people to correct me. I was able to visit a ton of places on the weekends when I was done with my schoolwork. I actually made a map of the places that I have visited between 2013-2015.
